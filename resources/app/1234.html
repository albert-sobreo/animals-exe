Keypoint

The type of movements of animals depends on their habitat.

Animal Movements

A. Animals that live on land move different ways. Some walk, hop, and jump. Others fly and crawl.

B. Most animals that live in water swim. But some aquatic animals have legs, too, for walkin e.g. crustaceans- shrimps, prawns, crabs.

C. Animals that can live both in water and on land can either walk, hop, jump, or swim.
